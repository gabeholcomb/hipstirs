Our story

More than just coffee

Hip-Stirs is Brookville's gathering place — a cozy spot where friendly faces greet you by name and every drink is made with real care. Owner Nick and his team have built a community hub where great conversations flow right alongside expertly crafted beverages. Inspirational scriptures on the walls, neighbors at every table, fresh ingredients in every cup.
